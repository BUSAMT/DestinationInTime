About Destination In Time

The Historical Developers Collective Time Travel Agency has assembled this site to allow the intrepid time traveller to find themselves inspiration in our Destinations. To start browsing, simply select an Era from the timeline above. If you wish to create a bespoke itinerary, then sign up (or login) from the navbar at the top of the page. From there you can navigate to My Itineraries and start creating a travel plan. Once you have an Itinerary you can get back to exploring. When you find a Destination that you are inspired by you can add it to one of your Itineraries.
